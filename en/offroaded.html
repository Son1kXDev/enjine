Offroaded

Version: 0.1.0 beta

Planned release date:

Developers: Enjine, DRAGOLITE Studio

Description

Immerse yourself in exciting virtual trips through the various maps of our game! Complete missions, improve and customize your car, buy new cars and make them unique, by customizing spoilers, splitters and suspension, as well as changing color and adding stickers. Don't forget to keep your car regularly serviced, fill it up on time and clean it from dirt after every exciting ride. Unlock many achievements and advance in the game by exploring various maps with different weather conditions.

Ready for a new adventure? Embark on our virtual trips today!

System requirements

Operating system: Android 5.0 Lolipop or above Windows 7 or above
Processor: ARM7 / ARM64 x64
RAM: 1GB or above 1GB or above
Free space: 200mb or above 200mb or above
Permissions: Access to photo library

Beta Features

  • Available only one map in three variations: day, night, rain
  • Available only one machine with a full upgrade package
  • Daily Missions, Achievements, Car Dealership and Garage unavailable
  • Bugs may be present
