About Enjine

Welcome to the world of Enjine - an independent game developer!

We specialize in creating games using Unity3D.

Currently, our portfolio includes three exciting projects:

  • "5opka Game" - a fantastic story based on a popular streamer in the Minecraft video game.
  • "Simulator of an Information Network Specialist" - a virtual reality simulator that allows you to experience the role of an information cable network specialist.
  • "Offroaded" - a Hill Climb Racing-style game for Android with improved car physics and tuning and painting tools.

We strive to create games that we ourselves enjoy playing.

We are open to collaboration and ready to share our knowledge and experience to help create the best games and make the gaming industry even more exciting.
