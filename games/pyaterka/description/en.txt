<p>A game with the main genre - shooter. You are immersed in the story of the main character - Kirill Baranov. This is the story of the protagonist's personality, and her struggle with the mistakes she made during her lifetime. The five deadly sins are lust, greed, envy, pride and anger, which disturb Kirill's soul and do not let the righteous go into the world in any way.</p><p>Will Kirill be able to overcome them? Or will it remain in eternal torment?</p>
